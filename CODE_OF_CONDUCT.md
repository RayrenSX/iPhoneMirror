# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Be respectful, technically honest and constructive. Welcome people regardless
of background or experience. Critique ideas and code, not people. Do not harass,
threaten, discriminate against or publish another person's private information.

## Project spaces

This policy applies to issues, pull requests, discussions, code review and other
spaces representing iPhoneMirror. Maintainers may edit or remove abusive,
off-topic, deceptive or privacy-violating content and may restrict participation
when necessary.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through their GitHub
profile. Security vulnerabilities belong in the private vulnerability reporting
channel described in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

Maintainers will consider context, impact and repeated behavior when responding.
