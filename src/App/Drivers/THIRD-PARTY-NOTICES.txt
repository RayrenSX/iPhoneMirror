libusb-win32 1.2.6.0
====================

The unmodified libusb-win32 1.2.6.0 filter driver and installer are bundled
for per-device iPhone USB capture support.

Project: https://sourceforge.net/projects/libusb-win32/
Release: https://sourceforge.net/projects/libusb-win32/files/libusb-win32-releases/1.2.6.0/

The kernel driver is distributed under GNU GPL v3 and the library/installer
under GNU LGPL v3, as stated by the upstream binary package.  The complete
corresponding upstream source archive is included as
libusb-win32-src-1.2.6.0.zip.  COPYING_GPL.txt and COPYING_LGPL.txt contain
the full license texts.  The files are used unmodified and carry no warranty.
The bundled kernel driver and DLL files have valid upstream Authenticode
signatures.  The upstream install-filter.exe is not Authenticode-signed;
iPhoneMirror verifies its exact SHA-256 before launching it from the compiled
elevated helper.
